01 · About

Hello, I'm Tanish

I am from Hyderabad. I completed my 11th and 12th in the Science stream, and my graduation in Computer Science Engineering from CBIT, Hyderabad.

After graduating, I worked two years at Deloitte USI Consulting, as an SAP Integration Developer. My work involves connecting with clients, understanding their requirements, and developing efficient technical solutions. I have been involved across different stages of projects, from requirement gathering to go-live and post go-live support, which has given me exposure to end-to-end project execution and client interaction.

For me, an MBA is the right way to bridge the gap between technical expertise and managerial expertise, enabling me to become a more well-rounded professional who can contribute not only through execution, but also through informed decision-making and leadership.That realisation brought me to XIMB.
